Our Story

We believe that your home should be your sanctuary—a reflection of who you are and how you want to live.

Intentional Design

Every piece starts with a sketch, a material study, and a real-life use case.

Modern Comfort

We balance strong silhouettes with soft textures and easy daily usability.

Built to Last

We focus on durability, repairability, and timeless form rather than trends.

Born from a desire for better living.

Aura Home was founded in 2021 by a collective of designers and architects who were tired of the fast-furniture industry. We noticed a gap between unattainable luxury and disposable flat-pack furniture.

Our mission is simple: to create beautifully crafted, sustainable furniture that doesn't cost the earth—in either sense of the phrase. Every piece is designed to last generations, not just seasons.

Our Core Values

Sustainability

We use FSC-certified woods and recycled materials whenever possible. Our carbon footprint is offset for every delivery.

Craftsmanship

Partnering with family-run workshops globally, we ensure fair wages and celebrate traditional woodworking techniques.

Timeless Design

We reject micro-trends. Our pieces are designed with a minimalist, timeless aesthetic that looks good in any era.
